What is Automatic build & deploy?

Forestry will watch your repo and automatically deploy your site whenever you commit an update (this is optional).

What is Forestry Remote?

Forestry Remote allows you to install the Forestry CMS on your site (mysite.com/admin) via a single html file. No updates required

How many sites can I set up

You can set up unlimited sites. If you are on the Business plan, you will be billed based on number of users in your organization.

Do I need to configure my project?

No configuration needed. Forestry will parse your project and automatically build your CMS.

What is a Guest?

A guest is a single-site user with limited permissions. Guests do not get access to support and are often used for clients or external colleagues.

What is a User?

On the Business plan, you can invite any number of users to your organization. Unlike Guests, users can access many sites and can be assigned a role (developer, editor, etc).

Does the Business plan include Guests?

Yes, the Business plan includes as many team members as you require ($9/month each) and also includes up to 3 Guest users per site.
